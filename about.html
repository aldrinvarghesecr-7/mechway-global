Supply Chain Since Day One

Mechway Global was established to connect globally recognized engineering manufacturers with the fast-evolving demands of project markets across the region. Our objective is to deliver reliable, compliant, and technically suitable mechanical products supported by strong product knowledge, responsive service, and efficient logistics.

Headquartered in Dubai, we serve a diverse range of projects across the UAE and the wider region, including MEP, infrastructure, industrial, marine, and selected oil and gas applications. Our portfolio includes piping systems, valves, flow-control solutions, and other specialized engineering products sourced through strategic partnerships with reputable manufacturers across Europe, the GCC, and Asia.

More than a product supplier, Mechway Global aims to be a dependable project partner. We work closely with contractors, consultants, developers, and end users to understand technical requirements, support product selection and compliance, and ensure materials are delivered in line with project schedules.

With a growing product portfolio and a strong commitment to quality, availability, and customer service, Mechway Global is positioned to support projects of varying scale and complexity across the region.

Our Mission

To empower MEP consultants, contractors, and developers across the GCC by delivering tier-one plumbing, mechanical, and HVAC products with zero logistical friction. We commit to bringing only fully certified, high-performance, and code-compliant engineering assets to the project site, ensuring every installation remains safe, efficient, and built to last.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and preferred supply-chain architecture for major MEP infrastructures in the GCC region, defining new industry benchmarks for technical submittal support, inventory availability, and value-engineered distribution.

Geographic Footprint & GCC Logistics Network

Mechway Global is strategically positioned to support fast-track projects with minimized lead times. Our logistical infrastructure is built to scale across the UAE and the wider GCC region, ensuring uninterrupted material supply to the most demanding project sites.
