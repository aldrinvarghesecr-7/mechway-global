Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Mechway Global ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, process, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage with our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of data:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and organization details provided when submitting forms, such as Requests for Quote (RFQs).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and usage details collected automatically through cookies and analytics tools.
  • Project Data: Technical specifications, documents, and bill of quantities submitted for project consultations.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • To process inquiries and generate accurate quotations.

  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations within the UAE and GCC.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your information only when necessary with:

  • Trusted logistics and manufacturing partners required to fulfill your specific project requirements.
  • Service providers who assist in operating our website or business (under strict confidentiality agreements).

5. Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to ensure your data is secure against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. We utilize encryption and secure servers to protect sensitive business inquiries.

Mechway Global operates primarily as a B2B service provider. You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data held by us. Furthermore, we explicitly state that we do not sell your personal information to any third party for marketing or monetary purposes.
